How to Use TypeRacer
  1. Select a difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. Click Start to begin the typing test.
  3. A countdown (3, 2, 1, GO!) will appear before you can start typing.
  4. Type the sample text exactly as shown in the prompt after "GO!" appears and the countdown closes.
  5. The timer will stop automatically when you finish typing the prompt, or you can click Stop to end the test.
  6. Your results (difficulty, time, and WPM) will be displayed below.
  7. Click Retry to try a new prompt and start again.
